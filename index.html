3D HOUSE VIEWS FOR CONSTRUCTION HELP

3D Building Modeling in a software (like 3ds max, autocad, revit, 3d home designer etc.) is a unique technology based computerised system in which we create a model of the building in a real shape and size for future with furniture, color, moulding, etc. and with great accuracy of dimentions in feet and inches, and according to the dimentions the building is ready for construction in its real shape and size inch by inch, and really the person who doesn't know what the front elevation and real structure, color will be in future of that building, knows everything with every aspect and every view of that building and construct the building structure of that building according to the vertical dimentions too of the building itself.

We create not only 2D plans and maps for the building to be constructed, but also create 3D views for that building with real dimentions and give a good plan in 2D and 3D. The difference between 2D and 3D is 2D building design is a map on the ground for marking the place to be constructed on whereas 3D is a real building computerised system created the building on that map and the walls and total structure on that 2D plan with accurate dimentions. . When construction starts, everyone knows the real structure of that building, what the building will be look like. Even the colomn and beam settings. So every type of real condition of the building appears in photos and videos in 3D.
